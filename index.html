About

Lorenzo Buoso is a Rome-based videomaker, specialized in documentaries.
He handles both shooting and editing, taking care of the entire creative process from concept to final cut.
His style is emotional and dynamic, aiming to deeply engage the viewer.
He currently collaborates with Alessandro Montesi, an Italian YouTuber and filmmaker with over 1.5 million followers, creating investigative documentaries for digital platforms.
Through his work, Lorenzo seeks to share his personal way of seeing and listening to the world — because he believes that’s the key to telling a meaningful story.
